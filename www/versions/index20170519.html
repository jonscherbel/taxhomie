Frequently Asked Questions

Should I get classic or volume eyelash extensions?

Classic eyelash extensions give you one synthetic eyelash per natural eyelash. Volume eyelash extensions give you a fan of synthetic lashes per natural eyelash for a fuller, fluffier look. Many people go with Hybrid (a mix of Volume and Classic) for an in between style at an in between price.

How long do eyelash extensions last?

About 2-4 of your natural eyelashes shed each day and are replaced by new natural eyelashes. Eyelash extensions will last as long as the natural lashes they are attached to with proper care. Generally a fill is required every 1-3 weeks to keep your lashes looking great.

How do I prepare for my eyelash appointment?

  1. Remove all oil near eyes (make-up, moisturizer, etc.)
  2. Set aside the appropriate amount of time for your service (listed under "Services and Prices")
  3. Remove contact lenses if your eyes dry out when closed for long periods of time

How do I take care of my eyelash extensions?

  1. Avoid steam, sweat, or water to eyes for 24 hours after appointment
  2. Avoid extreme heat; heat can melt/distort lash extensions
  3. Avoid oil-based make-up and make-up remover as oil breaks down the adhesive
  4. Wash your lash extensions with baby soap diluted with water 1-3 times per week depending on how much eye make-up you wear
  5. Do not use an eyelash curler or mascara on lash extensions
  6. Avoid rubbing your eyes and pulling on lashes
  7. Have your lashes filled every 1-3 weeks to maintain preferred fulness
